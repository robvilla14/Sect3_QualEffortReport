# Section 3 Quarterly Effort Report

A browser-based form for documenting a contractor's qualitative 
good faith efforts to comply with Section 3 of the Housing and Urban 
Development Act of 1968.

## What It Does
When contractors are unable to meet Section 3 labor hour benchmarks, 
they must document their outreach and hiring efforts. This tool 
captures those efforts in a structured, HUD-aligned format and 
produces a certification-ready PDF report.

## Who It's For
Contractors, subcontractors, and labor compliance consultants 
submitting Section 3 qualitative compliance documentation to 
housing agencies on federally funded projects.

## Features
- 18 effort checkboxes organized into three categories:
  Worker Outreach & Training, Resident Assistance, 
  and Business Concern Support
- Expand-on-check supporting notes fields — each checked effort 
  reveals a notes area for documenting specifics (dates, 
  organizations, participant counts, outcomes)
- Labor hours summary with live Section 3 and targeted worker 
  rate calculation
- Certification block with pre-filled HUD regulatory language
- PDF export with full findings layout and signature lines
- CSV export for record-keeping

## Compliance Reference
24 CFR Part 75 — Section 3 HUD Quarterly Reporting
Section 3 benchmarks apply to the greatest extent feasible. 
Qualitative documentation is required when quantitative benchmarks 
are not met.

## Usage
Open in any modern browser. No installation or internet connection 
required after initial load. Complete all applicable sections and 
export PDF before closing — form data is not saved between sessions.

## Export
PDF filename format: Section3_Effort_[ContractorName]_[Quarter][Year].pdf
CSV filename format: Section3_Effort_[ContractorName]_[Quarter][Year].csv

## Submission Note
Submit this report along with all supporting documentation including 
outreach postings, emails, public announcements, flyers, sign-in sheets, 
and any community outreach materials that evidence the efforts documented.

## Notes
This form captures contractor-level effort documentation. Section 3 
compliance is assessed at the project level across all contractors. 
For project-level hours tracking, use the companion tool: 
Section 3 Contractor Hours Dashboard.
